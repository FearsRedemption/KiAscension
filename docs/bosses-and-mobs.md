# Bosses And Mobs Plan

The current mod does not implement DBZ-flavored NPCs yet. This document records the intended direction and keeps future NPC work grounded in Terraria pacing.

## Planned Enemy Buckets

- Saibamen-style plant warriors for early training pressure.
- Alien scout and soldier enemies for invasion-style events.
- Tournament/training enemies for controlled sparring encounters.
- Bio-clone enemies for late pre-hardmode or hardmode pressure.
- Ki-trained invader mobs that teach dodging beams and barrages.

## Planned Boss Inspirations

- Early Saiyan invader: first real skill check after basic training.
- Saiyan invasion duo: pressure test for flight, melee, and early beams.
- Tyrant emperor: multi-phase ranged ki boss.
- Android/bio-android chain: hardmode scaling and mimic technique pressure.
- Majin destroyer: regeneration and chaos patterns.
- Legendary Saiyan superboss: optional aggression/endurance branch.
- God-tier destroyer: postgame movement and precision check.

## Integration Rules

- Bosses should be original, Dragon Ball-inspired encounters, not copied copyrighted characters or assets.
- Bosses should gate meaningful forms, techniques, accessories, training upgrades, or materials.
- Bosses should test the player’s current ascension tier instead of only being health sponges.
- Multiplayer should keep per-player progression and avoid granting unlocks client-side.

## Folder Direction

`NPCs/Bosses` and `NPCs/Enemies` are reserved for future implementation classes. Keep boss materials and drops aligned with `docs/items-framework.md`.
